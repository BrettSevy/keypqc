Services

Key Performance Quality Consulting works hard to provide a full complement of quality assurance services. Our team has direct, real-world experience in the following common and specialized areas:





Interim Quality Management

Some regulatory bodies and industry standards require a designated, trained quality professional be responsible for the quality management system at all times. Key Performance Quality Consulting can provide interim quality management in the event of a planned, or unplanned, gap in staffing.

GAP Analysis/Audit Prep/Internal Audits

Only through understanding risks to the quality management system can improvements be implemented; in quality assurance, these risks are identified through audits. Key Performance Quality Consulting can support your audit readiness in a variety of ways.

Quality Manual Creation

A well-written quality manual does more than just satisfy audit requirements. Key Performance Quality Consulting can help you develop a guiding document for your quality management system.

Food Safety/HACCP Plan Creation

The Hazard Analysis/Critical Control Point method of food safety plans is a proven, effective way to protect your customers. Key Performance Quality Consultants can provide end-to-end food safety, preventive controls, and HACCP plan development services.

Quality Engagement & Culture of Quality Training

Developing a culture of quality, where employees understand and embrace their role in customer satisfaction is critical to built-in quality. Key Performance Quality Consulting can develop and execute a plan to improve the culture of quality of your organization

Supplier Qualification & Supplier Auditing

Having confidence in your suppliers and raw-materials feeds confidence in your processes and, ultimately, your products. Key Performance Quality Consulting can develop a risk-based supplier qualification program in concert with your supply chain, product development, and operations teams.

Statistical Process Control Implementation

As well-intentioned as it may be, sometimes constant tinkering and adjusting can do more harm than good, increasing your process variation and creating nonconforming product to deal with. Key Performance Quality Consulting is an industry-recognized leader in Statistical Process Control implementation and can help you achieve the trifecta of happier operators, happier accountants, and happier customers.

Document Control & SOP Creation

Without sensible document control, even the best procedures are destined to fail eventually. Key Performance Quality Consulting can help you develop a document control program that fits your needs and works within your preferred medium, from paper-based to electronic

Data Analysis & Reporting

Data is only as good as the way it is analyzed and reported. Key Performance Quality Consulting can develop customized data analysis and reporting tools for your company. Leveraging PowerBI, Power Automate, and other industry-standard software solutions, real-time, automated reporting of data can be a reality.

Root Cause Analysis & Corrective Actions

The only thing worse than a costly quality miss is one that happens again. Key Performance Quality Consulting can provide root cause analysis and corrective action services to help implement truly effective solutions to recurring and emerging problems

Training Program Creation

The last thing your employees want to do after a long day on their feet is to sit through another training lecture. Key Performance Quality Consulting develops world-class, industry-recognized trainings built on principles of interaction, inclusion, and entertainment to deliver fun, exciting, and effective learning experiences. Let us show you how fun training can be!
