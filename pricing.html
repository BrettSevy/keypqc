Pricing

Every company has different needs, so Key Performance Quality Consulting meets those needs with a flexible pricing structure:

  • Project-Based - For projects with clear milestones, goals, or timelines, a project-based pricing structure can provide a transparent cost structure where the number of hours may not be immediately clear.
  • Retainer - When on-demand, ongoing services are required, a retainer-based pricing structure can provide consistency of service.
  • Hourly - No frills, fee-for-service pricing can provide an easy, straightforward pricing structure based on the number of hours worked

Key Performance Quality Consulting goes remote!

Leveraging today's technology, virtually all of our services can be performed remotely, providing our service quicker, safer, and on-demand. Inquire about pricing differences for remote services.
